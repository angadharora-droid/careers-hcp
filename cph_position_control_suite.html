Corporate Grade Structure

GradeMeaningPresent at CPA?Interview Panel

CPA (a full-service unit) has no A2/A4 positions in its sanctioned strength — shown honestly, not invented. Grades A1–A3 convene a 3-member committee; A4 and below use a 2-member panel.

PCN Format

CPA – DEPT – GRADE – SERIAL  e.g. CPA-FO-C1-001

Corporate Grade, Designation, Job Family, Business Unit, and PCN are separate concepts and never mixed. Two people at grade B1 can hold different designations, job families, and salary bands.

Recruitment Gate (position-first discipline)

A candidate can only be Selected into a PCN when: the position exists, its status is Vacant / Under Recruitment (not Frozen/Eliminated/Filled), and the required panel has scored. Selecting a candidate flips the PCN to Filled and records the occupant. This is the manpower discipline: no hire without an approved, budgeted, vacant position behind it.
